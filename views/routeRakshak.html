Route Rakshak

A MERN-stack civic-tech platform for real-time municipal ward ranking and complaint transparency in Amritsar.

Live Demo GitHub

The Problem

Municipal complaint systems often lack transparency, leaving citizens unaware of progress and councilors unaccountable for their response times.

The Solution

A centralized hub where 65 wards are ranked via a custom scoring engine, driving competition and clarity through data-driven governance.

The Scoring Engine

RouteRakshak implements a Dynamic Performance Ledger. Instead of simple averages, each ward's rank is determined by the cumulative actions of the authorities. This gamifies governance by rewarding resolution and penalizing delays.

GIS & Mapping Strategy

I used QGIS to digitalize ward maps, converting physical boundaries into GeoJSON data. This allows the MERN stack to render interactive, localized analytics for every specific ward in the city.
